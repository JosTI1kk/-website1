Bilor-ball

The character appeared by mistake, he is expected to be avoided by those who came for him, and he also needs to find his way back to his world!

The main character's name is Bilor or simply Bilo. He looks like a balloon (in his world everyone is like that, round, triangular, oval)

He lived on the harmless universe No. 17, but someone called him to another world.

your goal: to survive, explore the world to study it next time, get out of difficult situations, destroy opponents who attack you, and most importantly survive!

the game presents dangerous creatures that were ordered for you, it feels like you crossed someone path, but no! you just came from another world and they will be afraid of you, so find your way home as quickly as possible!

the game presents a dark location in which the world is saturated, but there are also bright places, its just hard to find them, its hard not to find them, but to get into them since darkness rules this world, there is one thing in despair, sadness, anger, fear, resentment, strength and weakness, and only the strong are in charge there, it was one of them who ordered your body as a trophy for the wall
